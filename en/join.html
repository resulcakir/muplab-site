Working in the lab

If you're curious about the neuro-physiological basis of learning, memory and behavior, you can join the MUPLAB team.

Who we're looking for

Undergraduate students

Volunteer research assistantship: data collection, experiment setup, literature and analysis.

Graduate applicants

MSc / PhD projects in psychophysiology, neuropsychology and cognitive neuroscience.

Researchers & collaborators

Reach out for joint work, data sharing and methodological collaboration.

How to apply

A short email is enough: introduce yourself, your interests and (if any) your CV. We'll get back to you — right away or when a suitable project opens.
